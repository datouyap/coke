About Us

Taste The Feeling


At Coca-Cola Malaysia, we offer more than 40 different beverages options across over 10 different brands. This includes our four colas, a range of sparkling drinks, waters, teas, juice drinks and sports drinks. We also offer lower- or no-sugar, no-calorie options for many of our drinks, as we work towards rethinking many of our recipes to reduce sugar and innovating to launch new lower or no-sugar drinks.

Products

Coca-Cola Classic

Coca-Cola Classic is the world’s favorite soft drink and has been enjoyed since 1886. Whether you want a 150ml mini can or a large 1.5 liter bottle for sharing, you can find Coca-Cola Classic in a variety of sizes to suit every lifestyle and occasion.

Coca-Cola Stevia

Coca-Cola Stevia is the latest addition to the 'Coca-Cola' family in Malaysia. It has 35% less sugar compared to Coca-Cola Classic, achieved by using a combination of sugar and the sweetness of Stevia.

Coca-Cola Zero Sugar

Coca-Cola Zero Sugar is a new and improved sugar free cola that looks and tastes even more like Coca-Cola Classic, but without the sugar. It replaces Coca-Cola Zero, which we launched in 2008, and is one of the steps we are taking to help people reduce their sugar intake from our drinks.

Coca-Cola Light

Coca-Cola light is a sugar and calorie free soft drink with a deliciously crisp taste. It was first introduced in the United States in 1982, and arrived in Singapore three years later, in 1985.

Coca-Cola Vanilla

Great tasting and uplifting, it is perfect drink to refresh you in the afternoon, with your meal or any time. Best served chilled for that ice-cold refreshing experience. Coca-Cola™ VANILLA delivers the great same great Coca-Cola taste with a Vanilla twist.
